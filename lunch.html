Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (about 225 grams) of spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn or chopped, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving (optional)

Simple steps and you are ready with your healthy and delicious Breakfast

  1. Cook Spaghetti: Boil salted water, add spaghetti, and cook until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté Onion and Garlic: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Sauté chopped onion until translucent, then add minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
  3. Add Tomatoes: Pour in crushed tomatoes and stir.
  4. Season and Simmer: Add sugar, salt, and black pepper. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Combine Pasta and Sauce: Toss cooked spaghetti in the tomato sauce until well coated.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle torn or chopped fresh basil leaves over the pasta. Optionally, top with grated Parmesan cheese. Enjoy!
  7. With these simplified steps, you can quickly prepare a delicious Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce and Fresh Basil dish.
