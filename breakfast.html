Veggie Salad

This meatless main-dish salad combines creamy, satisfying white beans and avocado. Try mixing it up with different seasonal vegetables.

                                       

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • ¾ cup veggies of your choice, such as chopped cucumbers and cherry tomatoes
  • ⅓ cup canned white beans, rinsed and drained
  • ½ avocado, diced
  • 1 tablespoon red-wine vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste

Simple steps and you are ready with your healthy and delicious Breakfast

  1. Combine greens, veggies, beans and avocado in a medium bowl.
  2. Drizzle with vinegar and oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Toss to combine and transfer to a large plate.
